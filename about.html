ABOUT ME

Who is Ranz?

Hi! I’m Ranzelle Paulino, a 21-year-old IT student at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela. I create original artwork like layout designs, vector illustrations, logos, simple animations, and video edits.

This website is where I showcase my projects, assignments, and creative experiments. Every piece reflects my growth, curiosity, and passion for creating visuals that feel expressive and intentional.

MY JOURNEY

Creativity started from curiosity.

Since childhood, I’ve loved editing. Without gadgets, I borrowed laptops just to experiment with editing software and creative tools.

During the pandemic, I explored vector art, layouts, branding, video editing, and even opened a small Shopee printing business.

I may still be learning, but every project became part of my growth — constantly experimenting, improving, and discovering new styles.
